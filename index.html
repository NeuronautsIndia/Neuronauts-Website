More About Us

At Neuronauts, we're passionate about shaping tomorrow's innovators through immersive, collaborative STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) experiences.

Our approach leverages cutting-edge technologies, including AI-powered LEGO robotics, advanced tinkering with IoT devices, machine learning, coding, AR/VR development and robotics engineering with drones and microcontrollers.

Our team of dedicated educators, engineers and technologists inspires curiosity and creativity through experiential learning, emphasizing problem-solving, critical thinking and real-world applications.

By fostering experimentation and exploration, we empower students to excel in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics), develop essential skills for the future and ignite a passion for innovation, creativity and lifelong learning.
